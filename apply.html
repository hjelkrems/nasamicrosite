Become a robot driver

Want to drive a NASA robot?

With this new project we are doing a test run where we invite 5 lucky people to come spend a week at the Goddard Space Flight Center near Washington, D.C. During this week they will get to learn how to control our old rovers that used to be on Mars, and then at the end of the week they will get to test and see the difference between a rover and our ANTS on a difficult terrain. It will be a week full of fun and learning and we encourage everyone to apply.

In the textbox, tell us about yourself and why you want to come spend a whole week with us. Must be 18 years or older
