Timeline for the ANTS

The Start

1 January 2003

The project starts. After much planning are the engineers ready to build the prototype for the TETwalker

Taking Shape

24 October 2004

After over a year of building, the prototype is roaming around on the floor at NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center

15 January 2005

Test Drive

The TETwalker is taken to McMurdo station in Antarctica to be tested. Here are some harxh conditions that can be similar to those on Mars

Time for nanos

30 August 2005

The prototype now works just as planned and the engineers are ready to work on the micro and nano moters so that the size can be a lot smaller.

ALI

2 February 2006

ALI (Autonomous Lunar Investigator) is proposed as a future mission for the ANTS technology. The mission is based on sending a TETwalker to the permanently shadowed Lunar polar areas. This place may contain resources, but is not available for humans to explore.

SARA

30 July 2006

SARA (Saturn Atonomous Ring Array) is proposed as another future mission. This Mission will look at Saturns ring

20 October 2006

Video

A video of how the TETwalker will function once finished was published. Take a look. (No sound)

TET WarFighter

19 October 2011

The TET warfighter is launched. To help in battles in the dessert. With a brand new AI that helps navigat through all obstacles.
